## 5. Sales Strategy

### 5.1 Service Offerings & Pricing

**Tier 1: Architectural Design**
- Residential: ₹500-800/sq.ft
- Commercial: ₹600-1000/sq.ft
- Custom luxury: Premium pricing

**Tier 2: Urban Planning**
- Master planning: Project-based
- Feasibility studies: ₹5-15L
- Zoning consultation: ₹2-8L

**Tier 3: Landscape Architecture**
- Residential gardens: ₹200-400/sq.ft
- Public spaces: Project-based
- Campus landscapes: ₹300-600/sq.ft

**Tier 4: Interior Design**
- Residential interiors: ₹1200-2000/sq.ft
- Commercial interiors: ₹1500-2500/sq.ft
- Consultation only: ₹50K-2L

**Tier 5: Sustainable Design Consulting**
- LEED certification support: ₹5-20L
- Energy audits: ₹2-8L
- Green building consultation: ₹100K-500K

**Tier 6: Project Management**
- Full-service PM: 8-12% of project cost
- Partial PM: 4-6% of project cost
- Consultation: ₹2-5L

### 5.2 Sales Process

**Stage 1: Initial Contact**
- Response time: <24 hours
- Qualification questionnaire
- Preliminary consultation (free)

**Stage 2: Discovery**
- Site visit (if applicable)
- Requirements gathering
- Budget discussion
- Timeline alignment

**Stage 3: Proposal**
- Concept presentation
- Detailed scope of work
- Pricing breakdown
- Timeline and milestones
- Team introduction

**Stage 4: Negotiation**
- Scope refinement
- Pricing adjustments
- Contract terms
- Payment schedule

**Stage 5: Closure**
- Contract signing
- Initial payment (30%)
- Project kickoff meeting
- Handoff to delivery team

### 5.3 Sales Targets

**Month 1-2 (Launch)**
- Inquiries: 50-100
- Qualified leads: 15-30
- Projects won: 2-5
- Revenue: ₹50L-₹1Cr

**Month 3-6 (Growth)**
- Inquiries: 100-150/month
- Qualified leads: 30-50/month
- Projects won: 5-10/month
- Revenue: ₹1.5-3Cr/month

**Month 7-12 (Scaling)**
- Inquiries: 150-200/month

- Revenue: ₹3-5Cr/month
