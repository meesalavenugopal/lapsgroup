# LAPS Logo Documentation

## Overview

The LAPS logo is a minimalist, architectural design inspired by the MIT logo style. It combines geometric bars with bold typography to create a memorable and meaningful brand mark.

---

## Logo Anatomy

### Visual Elements

**1. Four Vertical Bars (Icon Mark)**
- Each bar represents one letter of LAPS
- **L** (Landscape) - Full height bar
- **A** (Architecture) - Full height bar  
- **P** (Planning) - Shorter bar (approximately 68% height)
- **S** (Studios) - Shorter bar (approximately 68% height)

**2. Typography**
- **"LAPS."** - Bold, uppercase wordmark with period
- **Tagline** - "Landscape. Architecture. Planning Studios." in two lines
  - Line 1: "Landscape. Architecture."
  - Line 2: "Planning Studios."

---

## Design Symbolism

### Heights & Rhythm
- **Two tall bars (L, A)** represent the core pillars of the business
- **Two shorter bars (P, S)** represent supporting services and studios
- Creates visual rhythm similar to:
  - City skyline
  - Architectural elevation drawings
  - Building facades with varying heights

### Geometric Language
- **Vertical bars** = Columns/pillars = Foundation, strength, architectural precision
- **Clean lines** = Modern design sensibility, professionalism
- **Geometric precision** = Planning, structure, order

### Academic Authority
- Inspired by MIT logo = Credibility, innovation, excellence
- Institutional trust and academic rigor

## Usage Guidelines

### Spacing
- Maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of one bar
- Gap between bars and "LAPS." text: 26px (optimal balance)

### Sizing
- **Minimum width**: 200px for full logo with tagline
- **Minimum width**: 120px for icon + "LAPS." only (without tagline)
- Bars should maintain 20px width at standard size

### Typography
- **Main wordmark**: Arial Black, 58px, weight 900
- **Tagline**: Arial/Helvetica, 18px, weight 500
- Always include the period after "LAPS."

### Colors - Do's
✅ Use navy and gold on white/light backgrounds  
✅ Use white and gold on navy backgrounds  
✅ Use monochrome versions when brand colors aren't available  
✅ Maintain color alternation pattern in bars (L=Navy, A=Gold, P=Navy, S=Gold)

### Colors - Don'ts
❌ Don't change the color order of the bars  
❌ Don't use gradients or textures on the logo  
❌ Don't outline the bars or text  
❌ Don't use colors outside the brand palette
